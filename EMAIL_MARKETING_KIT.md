3.  **Google Maps Strategy:**
    *   Search "Trucking companies near me" or "Logistics centers in [City]".
    *   Visit their websites and look for "Contact Us" or "Careers" (often lists a hiring manager).

4.  **Hunter.io (Free Tool):**
    *   Type in a company domain (e.g., `swifttrans.com`) and it will show you the email pattern they use.

## üí° Best Practices for Cold Emailing

*   **Personalize:** Mention their company name or local area. Don't sound like a robot.
*   **Keep it Short:** Fleet managers are busy. Get to the point in 2-3 sentences.
*   **One Call to Action (CTA):** Ask for ONE thing (a reply, a trial download, or a 5-min call). Not all three.
*   **Follow Up:** 80% of sales happen after the 5th contact. Don't give up after one email!
*   **Track Opens:** Use a free tool like HubSpot or Mailtrack to see if they open your emails.
