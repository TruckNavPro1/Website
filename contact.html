Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription directly through your Apple ID settings on your iPhone under Subscriptions.

Does the app work offline?

TruckNavPro requires an internet connection for real-time traffic and routing updates, but maps are cached for short periods of offline use.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes, as long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID, you can use your subscription on multiple devices.
