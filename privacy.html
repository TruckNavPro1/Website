Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

TruckNavPro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

To provide navigation services, we collect and process your precise location data while the app is in use. This data is used solely for:

  • Providing turn-by-turn navigation
  • Alerting you to low bridges and restrictions
  • Recalculating routes based on traffic

We do not sell your location history to third parties.

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., app crashes, feature usage) to improve app performance and stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services
  • Send you updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Process transactions and manage your subscription via Apple App Store

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as map data providers or analytics tools) that collect, monitor, and analyze this type of information to increase our Service's functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
